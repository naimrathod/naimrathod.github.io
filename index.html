About

Who I am & how I work

Passionate about defending against evolving threats through proactive monitoring and incident response.

As an Associate Security Operations Specialist with a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Applications (BCA) and over two years of hands-on experience, I specialize in securing networks, web applications, and cloud environments across both offensive and defensive security domains..

I am a certified eLearnSecurity Junior Penetration Tester (eJPT) with practical experience in penetration testing and vulnerability assessments using tools such as Metasploit, Nmap, Nikto, DirBuster, Wireshark, Acunetix, Nessus, and Burp Suite. My offensive security background allows me to accurately identify real-world attack paths and validate vulnerabilities beyond automated findings..

On the defensive side, my work includes Web Application Firewall (WAF) management, log analysis, incident response, root cause analysis, and AWS cloud security. I regularly work with platforms such as Cloudflare, Imperva, Akamai, Fastly, AWS WAF, and cloud-native security services including AWS Inspector, GuardDuty, and Detective, along with SIEM solutions like Akamai WSA and the ELK stack..

I am a dedicated and driven security professional with a strong interest in ethical hacking, threat detection, and continuous improvement. I enjoy proactive monitoring and threat prevention as much as deep-dive incident investigations, producing clear, actionable reports, and helping teams strengthen their security posture through practical recommendations and security awareness training. I consistently seek to expand my knowledge and stay aligned with the latest cybersecurity threats, tools, and techniques.
